Things to Look For

—Security Plugins—

  • Ban Admin & sitename attemtps
  • Set Login Limits
  • 404 Detection
  • Email Notifications
If someone tries to log into your site with admin, knock them offline right away. You do not have those users? Limiting login attempts prevents brute force attacks at password guessing. If a bot is scanning for vulnerabilites on your site - out of date plugins/theme - they will get a high number of 404 errors. This is unusual. Block it immediately. Get notified when things are happening.
