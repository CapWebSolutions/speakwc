Lock Down Your Login Page

  • Two-factor authentication - 2FA
    —Works with Authy, Google Authenticator
  • Limiting login attempts
    • Local Brute Force Protection
    • Banned Users
    • 404 Detection
Two factor authentication prevents an intruder from getting into your site even if they crack your username and password. 2FA will send a code to you rsmart phone which you will then enter into the challenge box to complete the login process. I use this on my maintnance management tool as an added preventative measure.
Authy & Google Authenticator are good tools to use on your smart phone for authentication
Limiting logins is going to lock your login page down too, by the nature of their functionality.
